# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mongolian consumers shopping for vitamins, supplements, and nutritional products imported from the United States. 99% of traffic is mobile. Users are typically browsing during leisure moments, comparing products by brand and category, and expect a fast, frictionless path from discovery to checkout. Many are repeat buyers who value familiarity and trust.

## Product Purpose

Amerik Vitamin makes it easy for Mongolian customers to buy genuine US-imported vitamins and supplements with wide selection, competitive prices, and reliable delivery. Success is measured by conversion rate, cart completion, and repeat purchase frequency — not by time-on-site or content engagement.

## Brand Personality

Playful. Unique. Trustworthy.

The interface should feel like a confident local shopkeeper who knows their stock inside-out — not a faceless warehouse or a sterile pharmacy. Warmth and personality must come through the design without sacrificing clarity. Every interaction should reinforce that this is a curated, trustworthy source, not a generic dropshipper.

## Anti-references

- Generic minimalist black-and-white shadcn/ui lookalikes
- Cold, clinical pharmacy or medical-supply websites
- Templated Shopify storefronts with stock photography and generic trust badges
- Overly corporate supplement brands that bury products in lifestyle fluff

## Design Principles

1. **Conversion is the only metric that matters** — Every layout decision, CTA placement, and animation must justify itself by removing friction from the purchase path. If it doesn't help someone buy faster, it's clutter.

2. **Personality without noise** — The playful, unique brand voice should show up in typography weight, border treatments, and micro-interactions, not in decorative illustrations or distracting animations that slow down the mobile experience.

3. **Trust through transparency** — Price, stock status, brand origin, and delivery info must be immediately visible. Trust is earned by showing real information upfront, not by plastering generic "secure checkout" badges.

4. **Mobile is the real design surface** — Desktop layouts are secondary. Touch targets, thumb-reachable CTAs, fast scroll performance, and bottom-sheet patterns take priority over hover states or wide multi-column grids.

5. **Speed beats polish** — A slightly less beautiful page that loads in under 1s and renders immediately on a 3G connection will convert better than a pixel-perfect hero that takes 3s. Performance is a design principle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline
- Mobile-first touch targets (minimum 44×44dp)
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations
- Ensure sufficient color contrast on the warm yellow accent against both light and dark backgrounds
